### Decisions

- i18n is a controller-level transform: `verifyQrCode()` unchanged, cache language-neutral, `resolveMessageKey()` pure fn
- `PagedResult<T>` (new, `data`/`meta`) added alongside existing `PaginatedResult<T>` (old, `items`/`total`) to avoid collision
- `cooperative_id` (snake_case) already in `CurrentUserPayload` — Keycloak JWT claim already wired
- `user.cooperative_id ?? user.sub` fallback for dev safety
- `StreamableFile` + `passthrough: true` for binary download — NestJS idiomatic, no raw `res.send()`
- `findAndCount` added to test repo mocks (was missing in sprint-4 baseline)
